St. John's Wort, Ginko biloba & Tobacco - Cigarettes
by Mooseman

 
DOSE: 600 mg oral St. John's Wort (capsule)
  60 mg oral Ginkgo biloba (capsule)
    repeated smoked Tobacco - Cigarettes (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 185 lb


Over the last two weeks or so I’ve been taking two caps of 300mg St. John’s Wort Extract (.3% Hypericin), along with two 60mg Ginkgo caps for about a week. While it hasn’t made much of a difference in my daily mood other than a bit of anxiety relief from my hectic day-to-day college business (I’m usually not too depressed anyway), it has really affected the way my body deals with nicotine. I’d usually get a pretty good buzz from a cigarette at the end of a long day, but now…..nothing. It kinda sucks, because I’ve never really smoked much, maybe 2-3 cigarettes a day at most, and have had no intentions of quitting because I really enjoy the occasional cigarette, but now its as if I have no reason to, nor do I want to anymore other than to fulfill the need to have something in my mouth.
