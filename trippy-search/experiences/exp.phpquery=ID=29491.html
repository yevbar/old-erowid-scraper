I had never tried Somas before, only heard tales of this mysterious drug. I stumbled upon a full bottle of the 350mg pills when my friend complained to my dad of a soar hand. He had punched something and broke it the night before while he was extremely intoxicated. So I get the bottle, come online and type in 'CARISOPRODOL', to learn more about the drug. Seemed very Codiene like so I popped about 4 of them at noon. After a little while I felt nothing, so out of frusteration I popped 4 more at 12:30 and my friends and I decided to go to the hot tub at my grandfathers country club. That's where my memory of that whole day ends.

We didn't have a key to get in, so we broke in and upon entering the hot tub there was 2 cops watching us in the hot tub... we were so fucking out of it that we didn't even know and carried on with our business. I was incoherient and the cop asked me how we got in here and I just remember telling him that I didn't want to talk to him because I was very 'tired'. Somehow, the cop got my moms phone # out of me or one of my friends and called her and told her that I was unconcious in the country club hot tub. She told him to send my ass home ASAP.

So my buddy drove us all home (I guess?) and I got carried into my house and my mom told me I face planted in the garage and it was scary to see me so wasted. I passed out for several hours, word is I was foaming at the mouth. I slept it off, never seeked any medical treatment and woke up around 8:00pm or so and began walking around my house blindly. I knew I had to pick my girlfriend up from the air port so I got in the car and went. [Erowid Note: Driving while intoxicated or tripping is dangerous and irresponsible because it endangers other people. Don't do it!] The lines on the road were blurring... it was like I was tripping. The lines kept twisting into each other and I kept swirving all over the place, it was crazy.

To say the least I didn't really have a pleasurable experience with this drug. It's like 3 days later and I still feel all groggy and weak... I think due to the overdose. Later.
