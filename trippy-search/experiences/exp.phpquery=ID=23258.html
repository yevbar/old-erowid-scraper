My Other Life
Salvia divinorum
by LoKi007

 
DOSE:   repeated smoked Salvia divinorum (extract - 5x)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


After getting done with another geat Salvia trip I am beginning to see a definite pattern in what I experience. Everytime without fail I get into this 'other' life. The same one every time and I feel ashamed of myself when it starts to go away.

I always feel like I am neglecting this other life when I start to come down. There are the same strange beings that are always there and the same type of occurences. It always take place in front of this garage and I think I'm working on this car... fixing the bumper. It's always seem like another normal life that I can only feel when I'm on Salvia. I never think I am on the drug when I am feeling this but I always do have this feeling of fleeting. I know it doesn't last long and always wish I could stay (when I'm on it). I just think it's kind of funny that it can be the same every time unlike any other hallucinogen I have ever tried where every time is always a different experience.
