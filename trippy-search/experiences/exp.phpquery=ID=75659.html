Weaker Than I Expected
Salvia Divinorum Tea (5x extract)
by Eruntero

 
DOSE: 0.5 g oral Salvia divinorum (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 65 kg


I decided to try salvia tea. I put 1g of 5x extract to boiling water kept it boiling for 10 minutes. I made up 2 cups. I gave one to my brother and drank one by myself. I was very bitter, so I put some honey into it.

After few minutes I felt somehow relaxed and clean-minded. After about 10 next minutes I begin to feel a bit sleepy. But this was weak and could possibly be even produced with placebo effect.

I expected it to be much stronger.
