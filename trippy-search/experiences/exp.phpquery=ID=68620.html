The Edges of Psychedelia
Piperazines - BZT & TFMPP
by DavEy

 
DOSE: 140 mg oral Piperazines - BZP (pill / tablet)
  68 mg oral Piperazines - TFMPP (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


I was a bit put off by the experiences I was reading until I finally tried it myself. It is described by many as similiar to MDMA, when it's really closer to mescaline or 2C-I perhaps. My greatest effects were between three and six hours, I felt warm and peaceful and energetic, on the edges of psychedelia (as with MDMA).
