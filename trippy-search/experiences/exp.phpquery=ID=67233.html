11:30 pm - I felt a little high so I took another.

11:45 pm - Holy crap I was high. It was amazing. The high was better than any I had from smoking and I didnt smell like pot, which I thought was a great plus.

12:00 am - I got incredibly hungry and the worst dry mouth ever.

My high lasted until around 2 or 3. Was an amazing experience.
