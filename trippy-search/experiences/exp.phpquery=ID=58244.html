Kratom
by Bob

 
DOSE: 1 tsp oral Kratom (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


In college I never even touched drugs or alcohol. But after 25 years in the work force I just got worn down.

I noticed my consumption of alcohol was getting out of hand so I thought I would try something different.

I was very skeptical especially after a brief time with prescription drugs.

I read about Kratom and just thought I would try it. I ordered some and made some tea out of the concentrated powder. It was nasty tasting but downed it and after a while a tremendous sense of contentment swept over me and I could rest without worrying about something. My dog hopped up to great me and for the first time in about 5 years I was very happy.

I slept and rested about 12 hours!! It is usual for me to only experience maybe 6-7 hours of rest a day.

Very very nice!
