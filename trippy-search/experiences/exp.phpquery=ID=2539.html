You can definitely taste that Opium was present after smoking the bowl/pipe with just smoking weed. But today, I'm submitting a real Opium experience without any other drug, so I took a few pictures (So they can be added to the Erowid archive) of the opium rock I had left and in a few moments I'll begin to smoke and experience what a real Opium high is without the weed. I'll be smoking it out of a glass bowl, with a screen in it, and I also cleaned out the bowl. Right now I'm fully sober, and feel completely fine. Not for too long. I originally bought 1 Gram of Opium in rock form for $15. This is about 1/4th what was left, which is what I'll be smoking.

(No I've never smoked an incense... :p the taste reminds me of the smell if a light smelling incense) My mind is at peace right now. It's almost like smoking weed for the very first time. I'll put some music on loud to see how that is. My ears are very sensitive over sudden, music is wonderful... I'll be back in about 20 minutes to add more.

20 Minutes Later :

Music is so wonderful, I'm so mellow. I can't put the music loud enough, I want the music to engulf me. In the last ten minutes I put on a program that danced with the music. I prefer weed or shrooms. :) It feels like my head is completely empty without a worry in the world, I love drugs.
