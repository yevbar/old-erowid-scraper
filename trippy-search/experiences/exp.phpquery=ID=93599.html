Abusing Inhalers
Albuterol
by MidnightToker

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 5 hits inhaled Pharms - Albuterol
  T+ 0:30 12 hits inhaled Pharms - Albuterol

BODY WEIGHT: 170 lb


At first I took 5 hits of Albuterol Sulfate, which I am prescribed because of sports induced asthma. I was lying down when I took them. I felt a slight buzz, comparable to nicotine, for about 30 seconds. I waited 30 minutes and then I had 12 to 15 hits. This time I was sitting up. I felt a much stronger buzz for a minute. 10 minutes later I noticed some chest pains. My lungs felt very tight. This feeling was off and on for the rest of the night. Now it is the next day, and I feel fine. No permanent damage was done, but huffing Albuterol was an awful idea.
