Ziprasidone (Geodon)
by Greg

 
DOSE: 20 mg oral Pharms - Ziprasidone (daily)
    oral Pharms - Alprazolam (daily)
    oral Pharms - Sertraline (daily)
    oral Pharms - Bupropion (daily)


I recently started taking geodon. Almost 15 minutes after I popped my fist pill I felt the effects. There was tingling feeling in my arm and legs. When I sat down my feet wanted to move. It felt great. I couldn't stop smiling and I was laughing at serious things that are not funny.

I found it very helpful in making me relax. I got the best feeling from it when I lay down. I can feel the tingling in my back and arms and legs and I am giddy and stupid with laughter.

Every time I take it I have similar effects. The only problem I have with geodon is when it completely wears off which is usually 4 p.m the next day. When it wears off I find myself in an uncontrollable rage. All I need to do though is take one geodon and the rage is gone in five minutes.

I am on Geodon with Xanax and Wellbutrin and Zoloft.
