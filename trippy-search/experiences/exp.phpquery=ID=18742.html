A Very Pleasant Ride
2C-C
by BLAZE

 
DOSE: 30 mg oral 2C-C

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


Just a really pleasant experience with NO negative efffects a nice come down and a refreshed feeling after sleep.

The dose was tried at this level due to the fact this compound had not been tested previously in our research circle. I am positive now that next trials will be somewhere between the 40-55mg mark. This was a thouroughly enjoyable experience however with hightened senses all around. The appreciation of art, music, touch, taste and smell and incredibly effective at de-stressing. I can see the 25-35mg mark being very usefull for public activities and enhancement of appreciation of many things. The mood was very humerous and good spirited.

Effects took approximately 1.2-2hours to really develop with the plateau starting at or around the 2.5-3 hour mark lasting about 2 hours then tapering off to a very relaxed state and drifting off to a very refreshing sleep. Awoke feeling VERY refreshed and with no negative effects. The whole experience is very relaxed with very mild stimulation.

I think this can and will be a very useful theraputic tool in the future. It had much to offer with nothing really negative at all. At this dose level at least. I look forward to future trials with much anticipation.

Much investigation is needed.
Another valuable Phenethylamine with much to offer!
