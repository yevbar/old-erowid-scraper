No Effect Whatsoever
Salvia officinalis (Common Sage)
by Please Be Original

 
DOSE: 0.5 joints/cigs smoked Salvia officinalis (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


So I was bored one day and decided to smoke something. I couldn't quite think of anything that would be good for smoking until I remembered that I had some sage leaves in my kitchen cupboard.

So anyway, I took a bunch of dried leaves and sprinkled them into half a sticky note with crused wet paper as a filter, rolled up the note and lit up. The taste was sweet but harsh. I smoked the whole thing down and then threw out the 'filter'. I sat for about two hours and noticed nothing at all. Everything seemed to be perfectly normal.

Synopsis: It's a pleasent tasting herb but has no effect.
