V8 Double Shot
Morning Glory Seeds
by Anonymous

 
DOSE: 1 oz oral Morning Glory (seeds)


I found a good way to ingest morning glories; I used this when I experimented with the seeds. At first a friend and I sat down with an ounce of untreated seeds and began chewing small 'pinches' thoroughly and swallowing. This was fine at first, but after about 200 seeds the taste was unbearable, I would have puked if had chewed any more.

My friend, who is obviously much tougher than I am, finished his half ounce in one sitting without mixing them. An hour later he began his trip and felt nothing but
nausea. I knew I had to finish the other quarter ounce that was left for me to get to his level. So, I ground the seeds in a grinder until they were mostly powder and then poured a double shot of V8 bold and spicy.

I then poured the powdered seeds on top of the 'double shot' and slammed it down.
The V8 was so thick and overwhelming that I couldn't even taste or feel the texture of the seeds. The end result: an hour later I was up there with him and we spent the evening tripping out of minds. It was a great experience. Next time I'm not even messing around with chewing the seeds.I'll just make up several V8/seed shots and ingest it that way.
