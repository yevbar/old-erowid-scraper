Fundamental #17: Seek treatment if needed.
Not a very good idea
2C-T-7
by TheOtherSide

 
DOSE: 10 mg smoked 2C-T-7 (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


I tried to smoke the HCL form out of a bowl sprinkled over some marijuana but it wasn't any good. This time I decided I would vaporize the freebase form. I measured out 10mg of the HCL and put it in my vaporizer. Now I dropped a drop of ammonia over it and set it aside to evaporate. After it was all gone I turned on the torch and got ready to smoke. I allowed it to fill up as much as possible with the vapor then quickly inhaled. I hadn't even inhaled half of it and I was forced to blow it all out. It was the same terrible burning it gave me when I snorted it but this time I couldn't use the Lidocaine.

It keeped getting worse for the next five minutes or so. It hurt quite a bit to breath. This defenately wasn't good. Eventually, after about 30 minutes I started to feel a little better. The pain went away and I noticed I was tripping a bit. It was a very mild trip with little visuals. It actually felt pretty good but this seems like a a sure way to die. I strongly discourage everyone from using this route of ingestion.

Be careful, know your body.
