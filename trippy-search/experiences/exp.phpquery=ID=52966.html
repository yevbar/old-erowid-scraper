Calamus
by Wharfrat

 
DOSE: 6 in oral Calamus (roots)

BODY WEIGHT: 170 lb

Bad idea. I read some experiences online but decided to try it anyway, thinking I had a strong stomach. Bad idea. Bad idea.

My goodness! I have been vomiting for 6 hours now and cannot sleep.

I feel like checking myself into some sick mental health clinic. What the &^$%$ was I thinking!
