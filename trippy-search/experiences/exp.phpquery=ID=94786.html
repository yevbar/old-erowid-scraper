Smoked A. colubrina
Anadenanthera colubrina
by EntheoAssassin

 
DOSE: 2 seeds smoked Anadenanthera colubrina (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 84 kg


I was feeling anxious. I was anticipating an experience. Just not sure which type.

I heated the three seeds in a pan until they 'popped' and then de-husked them. I ground up three seeds and loaded just over half of it into a water pipe with a little bit of cannabis.

Immediately I felt a headache feeling...and then a pressure that led to nausea. Then the a very familiar body feeling not unlike DMT.

No closed eye visuals. Just nausea and that familiar body feeling.

In summary, for 84kgs body weight, 2 seeds was probably not enough. Will try 3 in future and report here. Will also try combining with Syrian rue seeds, and also via snuff.
