The Blue Mouth
Flunitrazepam (Rohypnol)
by Underground Pharma

 
DOSE: 1 tablet oral Pharms - Flunitrazepam

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


As an underground pharmacist who rarely faces life unaided by some sort of chemical or another I was eager to try Rohypnol after years of hearing about it.

The first thing I noticed was funny. Since this is the 'date rape' drug when I broke the army-green caplet in half I saw that they have dyed the inside dark blue with some sort of vegetable dye. I didn't notice this the first time and chewed one up only to walk downstairs to spend time with my wife, kids, my parents and their guests. 'Have you been chewing on a pen?' Of course, my mouth was dyed blue.

I've taken them off and on and honestly must say that I find them disappointed. I have felt very little with them and believe that 10mg of diazepam or .5mg of Xanax beats them by a mile. I did, however, avert a minor panic attack once by quickly chewing and dissolving one of these blue devils under my tongue. Effective and pleasant that time.

In the end they feel like a waste to me. I'd rather buy Valiums and Xanax.
