A Very Nice Recreational Drug
Trazodone
by Sin

 
DOSE:   insufflated Pharms - Trazodone (ground / crushed)

They're a bit harsh but sort of sweet as well. They mix very well with a few beers or a little to drink...very nice sedative feeling...relaxation. No adverse side-effects for me.
