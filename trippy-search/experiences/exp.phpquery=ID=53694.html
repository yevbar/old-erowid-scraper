Sweet Sleep
Mirtazapine (Remeron)
by Eazy E

 
DOSE:   oral ?? (daily)


I have used Remeron a lot to help me sleep. It helps some with depression although I found that meditating and praying to God helped my depression more.

Anyways, here is what I have experienced on remeron.

First of all crazy dreams, very intense and vivid.

Some cravings for sweets when on it, it makes me hungry sometimes.

When I started taking it the remeron makes me sleep for like 24 hours straight.
