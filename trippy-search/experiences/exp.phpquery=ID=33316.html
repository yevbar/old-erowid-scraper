Heart Problems/Numbness
Cannabis
by Nick H.

 
DOSE: 2 bowls smoked Cannabis (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


Yea I had a very bad problem once. Me and my friend smoked a couple gravitys (a container to hold smoke in water to push smoke into lungs) of some chronic $50 an 8th, I was ok when my heart started racing really bad to point I thought it was going to explode. My chest tightened and I started to get scared to where I was walking it off and controling my heart.

That wasn't as bad as the recent experience. I have smoked for a bout 3 years now mostly chronic, and it seems getting high has lost intrest aka negative effects. Today I smoked 2 bong packs of some good weed, I was getting a powerful buzz through out my body. Then it happened my heart beats got weaker and weaker. I thought I was dying or something. The tighting of the chest happened and my hearing faded and rang, my tongue went numb and the arms and feet as though as I felt my body dying. I started to move real quickly and to get my heart beat up. It took like 30 mins to feel like I was alive again. I made it through that buzz killer but now I'm scared of smoking weed again. It haunts me already of that moment where I thought I was going to die of 2 bong packs.
