A Trip to the Hospital
750 mg DXM, 600 mg Diphenhydramine
by Anonymous

 
DOSE: 750 mg oral DXM (liquid)

I chugged the whole bottle of dxm at once, and swallowed all the pills, then waited for the effects to come up. The time was 9:45 pm when I took them.

I do not remember what happened, except for the fact that I woke up in a hospital. My sister helped me fill in the blanks when I talked to her the following day.

09:45 PM- Took 750mg dxm and 600mg dph

My sister got worried and woke up my mom, explaining to her the situation. They call the hospital to see what their options are. They can either call the cops, take me into emergency, or take me to the hospital.

My mom tells me that we are going for a ride, and I got up exitedly, not even wondering why we would go for a ride at 2am. We get to the hospital, and I go outside telling my mom and sister that I am waiting for a friend of mine, they tell me to wait inside because it is cold.

In the hospital, I am hearing voices of my friends, and I am still not in coherence. I asked my mom for zig zags several time, and she gave me a tissue paper, which I rolled. My mom gets me to drink the charcoal by telling me that its beer. I finally settle down and goto sleep. I woke up at 6:30am, and we went home.

This was a scary experience which only happened 3 days ago. I am writing this to warn people to be careful with otc drugs, and stick to marijuana. If my sister hadnt seen how fucked up I was I could've died.
