Hit by a Train
Zolpidem (Ambien)
by Helperof others

 
DOSE: 10 mg oral Pharms - Zolpidem (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I took ambien one night and feel asleep. Next thing I know I was hit by a train in my car in a train yard. I was sleep driving. It happened for real and my memory of the event is like a dream. It totalled out my car. I lived to tell the horrible side effect story of ambien. Don't do it. It can cause serious problems.
