Pain is Managable
Wild Lettuce (L. canadensis)
by Don

 
DOSE: 3 Tbsp oral Lactuca - L. canadensis (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 275 lb


As I read these reports, I see a lot of people looking for a buzz!

I suffer chronic neck pain and have had two surgeries with only minimal success. I've been looking for alternatives to prescription drugs and think I may have hit on something. I've just finished two cups of tea made with a total of 3 tablespoons of dried plant. I feel very relaxed and the pain is very managable.

I am thinking of combining this tea with a cold infusion of kava. Next time.
