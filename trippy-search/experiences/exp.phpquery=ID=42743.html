Yawn
Barbiturates (Phenobarbital)
by a human being

 
DOSE: 500 mg oral Barbiturates - Phenobarbital (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 250 lb


I have a fairly regular supply of phenobarbital, and I take advantage of it. My usual dose is 500 mg and that suits me well. 300 will do, 1000 is the limit for me.

Every expirience is about the same. Heavy sedation, lots of yawning, very little thinking, slow reaction time and terrible motor skills are things to expect. It's like being extremely tired except coffee wont pick me up.

Its a very fun drug, but not one to do the day before something important, and it can be addictive if taken on a regular basis.
