Disallusioned Disorientation
Amanitas - A. muscaria
by Sabrielle

 
DOSE: 3 caps oral Amanitas - A. muscaria (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


I recieved 28grams of Amanita Muscaria through the mail and was excited to try the world's oldest ethneogen. Around 11:30pm we consumed about 3 medium sized caps each and were eagerly anticipating the effects. Around 30 minutes later there was definitely a change in sensation, objects seemed brighter, surreal, but felt like you were on the prespice of an intense experience.

Unfortunately this was as good as the experience got. My partner started to get red blotches on his face and went to the bathroom to hurl. He appeared to be having an allergic reaction to the mushrooms he threw up 3 times before the night was over and was not at all happy with the effects. I felt somewhat as if I was going to hit a psychadelic state, but that never seemed to occur.

The next morning I felt as if I had a hangover and my head hurt--interaction with people was very difficult and I felt nauseated, it was hard to concentrate on anything. All and all I felt I wasted the money and time on this and it was not what I had expected, I don't think I'll be trying these again.
