Problems with Math
Tramadol
by Jeffy

 
DOSE: 300 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 195 lb


The most note-worthy of effects is that I experienced extreme difficulty in urinating up to 20 hrs after taking the pills. But I did figure out a way to get around this problem. When I knew that I had to urinate, but just couldn't, I would go into the bath tub and let warm/hot water run on my genitals. After a few seconds of this I was able to urinate freely.

I am not experienced with opiates, opiods, and pain killers, so I don't know what normal after affects are, but I will note that I seem to be less mentally agile the next day(I am an aspiring mathematician and I found it quite difficult to read mathematical or scientific literature after taking tramadol for a day)
