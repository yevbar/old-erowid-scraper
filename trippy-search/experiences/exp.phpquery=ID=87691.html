Calm Happiness
Morphine
by lokicola

 
DOSE: 30 mg oral Morphine (pill / tablet)
    repeated smoked Cannabis  

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


At around noon one day I decided to drop a 12 hour slow-release morphine tablet as I had several times before.

It was an amazing feeling as usual, like someone giving me a full body massage and freeing me of any pain.

Later after I had gotten off work at 5, the effects of the morphine in full swing at this point, I decided to smoke a little blunt.

As I smoked I could feel any and every amount of tension release with my exhales as I was overcome with a sense of warmth and security.

This was one of the most intense highs I had ever experienced that lasted the whole day.
