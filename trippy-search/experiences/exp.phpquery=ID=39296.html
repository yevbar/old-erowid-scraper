Cushioning the Drop-Off
Amphetamines (Adderall)
by Adderall Expert

 
DOSE: 60 mg oral Amphetamines (pill / tablet)
    oral Vitamin C (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 96 lb


I take Adderall for ADD/mild autism, and unfortunately for me, my brain needs a much higher dose than my kid-sized stature can handle.

As soon as I start 'coming down' from the Adderall, I take a bunch of Vitamin C tablets, probably 4-10 (1000mg). I usually was looking at about 2 to 6 hours each day (yuck) of dealing with the wearing off effects, but I haven't had to deal with it at all since I figured out this nifty little trick.
