In an Odd State
Methadone
by Pill Bug

 
DOSE: 20 mg oral Methadone (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


Well as far as Methadone goes it's fun. Once I get past the shitty throwing up phase it'll rock my world and kick my ass. If I take it in the morning it'll last me all damn day. I take them in pill form, so it's time released.

The thing w/ Methadone is that when I take them it's normally at night. I still get a little sick to my stomach, and normally I throw up at one point or another, but once I do I feel great and it won't bother me again. Anyhow, I usually take them at night and they kick my ass. LOL... I'll be sitting there in a weird state of I'm here but I'm not here. It's fun.

By the next day I get up and basically feel shitty the entire next day. That kinda sucks. I think that's just me though.. All of my friends eat them and they have no problem with that.
