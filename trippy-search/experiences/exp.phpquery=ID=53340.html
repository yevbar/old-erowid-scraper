Euphoria That Doesn't Just Last a Few Hours
Methadone
by Count Orlok

 
DOSE: 10 mg oral Methadone (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


Having tried just about every opiate I could ever get my hands on I'd always wanted to experience methadone. I chewed a 10 mg tablet and waited for maybe an hour and a half to get high. I think 10 mgs is a good starting dose for someone that has no opiate tolerance.

12 hours later I still have a buzz. I can't see how this isn't a more prevalent street drug.
