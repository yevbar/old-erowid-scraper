Childhood Memories
Piracetam
by Ryan J

 
DOSE: 2400 mg oral Piracetam (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I have been taking Piracetam for a month, now and have enjoyed its subtle, immediate effect of a certain stillness of the mind. After about a half hour or so, at 2400mg, a creeping calm begins its flow over me and I feel very in touch with all that is around me.

With a healthy intake of Kerouac, Jon Krakauer, and Hunter Thompsons books, I must say that this stuff seems to synergize all of these notions and ideas that have been floating around in my head.

Also, I have been finding that throughout the day, I have gotten wisps of old long forgotten memories from my childhood. Its pretty absurd, and I laugh when it happens, but I'm starting to think that it may have something to do with the Piracetam.
