Beauty and Freedom
2C-I & 5-MeO-DMT
by The Mime

 
DOSE: 5.0 mg insufflated 5-MeO-DMT (powder / crystals)
  60 mg insufflated 2C-I (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


[Erowid Note: Driving while intoxicated or tripping is dangerous and irresponsible because it endangers other people. Don't do it!]

[Erowid Note: 60 mgs of 2C-I is considered an extremely high dose, see 2C-I Dose Chart ]

My friend recently ordered a gram of 5-meo-DMT and a Gram of 2C-I. He offered me to be the test bunny of snorting the DMT, which I was not to sure about from my previous experience with smoking DMT.

I snorted about 5 mg of the DMT. I have read that this stuff is supposed to burn like hell, but I felt no burn. Before the effects had kicked in he started capping out his 2C-I. I gave him thirty dollars and he cut me out a 60 MG line of 2C-I. With no comprehension of what I was about to get myself into I slammed it up my nose.

The burn came immediately followed by the intense urge to go to his van and lay in the back until the burn subsided. I felt as though I had been punched in the nose by Mike Tyson in his prime, and the rage of being hit was right there with it, so being smart I removed myself from the presence of people.

The visuals came quickly. It was a rainy day and the water drops were dripping slowly down his window. The stickers in his van had come to life. I was so comfortable but so irritated that I had this pain in my nose.

Finally the neg. effects stopped and we decided to get in a car and go to my other friends house. Going down the street at about 90 mph I rolled my window down and stuck my head out. It was like I was next to the car not in it. I was looking at the glow of everything around. The rain drops hitting my head at that speed didn't sting rather it was an indescribable feeling. The only words I could muster were beauty and freedom.
Me and my friend M spent a long time walking into stores then quickly running out in hysterical laughter cause we couldn't take how strategically placed everything was. We chased after a dog.

My personal favorite part of the trip was when I wandered into the woods to discover Amanita Muscaria's everywhere. I have a strange connection with these mushrooms sober or tripping. I appreciate their beauty and their power so it was fun to see them in this frame of mind.

Eventually we got to a house and relaxed. We spent the rest of the night taking hits of 5-meo-DMT and laughing. The whole thing probably lasted about 10 to 12 hours.
