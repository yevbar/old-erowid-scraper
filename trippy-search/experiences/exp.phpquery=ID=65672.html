Fundamental #9: Choose an appropriate occasion and location when using a psychoactive drugs.
Adderall In Verse
Amphetamines (Adderall)
by Rachel

 
DOSE: 60 mg   Amphetamines

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


Adderall has taken away my soul
I felt so brilliant at first
Capable of anything
I could just go
and go
and do
so much
people were envious of me
because I never stopped
never crashed
ever
And my drive
my drive
I had such discipline
I was strong
focused
dedicated
confident
and I was so
so
so
alive
everything that came out of me was golden
Crisp.
Clean.
Sharp.
Perfect.
I felt success
It was coming
out of my pores
surrounding everything I did
I was absolutely glowing.

But now I’m just numb
Stuck
Hollow
I can’t finish a feeling
My thoughts stop and start
and I don’t really remember who I am
anymore.
