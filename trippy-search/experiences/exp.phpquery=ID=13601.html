A Waste of Time
Ginkgo biloba
by Anonymous

 
DOSE: 1.0 g oral Ginkgo biloba (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


It was about 8:00p.m and I was bored so I decided to see what gingko biloba could do to me if I took a high dosage. I went to my medicine cabinet and found a box of pills of gingko biloba. They were 100mg pills. I took 10 of them, wanting to see if I could get an effect. All that happened was that I felt a little more energetic and was more clear minded. I went to sleep then.


I woke up in the morning, got ready for school, and so on. I had weight- lifting this morning. During lifting I felt a little less tired then I usually am. Nothing more happened through the day. One thing that I noticed was that my memory was quite well.
