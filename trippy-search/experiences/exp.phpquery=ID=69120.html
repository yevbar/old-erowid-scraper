Fundamental #14: Modify psychoactive drug use if it interferes with work or personal goals.
Works Well For My Insomnia
Trazodone
by Kat

 
DOSE: 100 mg oral Pharms - Trazodone (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 250 lb


I have been taking Trazodone off and on for insomnia or about 7 years. It works well, is not addictive, and has no recreational use. However, it gives me absolutely insane dreams. I normally have vivid dreams but the nightmares with Trazodone are more intense. But hell, at least I sleep.
