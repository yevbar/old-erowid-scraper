Pina Coladas
Cacti - T. pachanoi (San Pedro)
by Sillycybin60

 
DOSE:   oral Cacti - T. pachanoi


It has been my experience, that after you use your favorite extraction method, if you freeze the liquid in ice trays you can then make frozen drinks. My wife and I like to make pina coladas. This makes it easy to drink with no nausea. You may experience one small problem; brain freeze.
