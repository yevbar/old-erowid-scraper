Seroquel (Quetaiphe), Imipramine & Bupropion (Wellbutrin)
by WatersMoon110

 
DOSE: 200 mg oral Pharms - Quetiapine (daily)
  275 mg oral Unknown (daily)
  400 mg oral Pharms - Bupropion (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 300 lb


I take these three medicines, as prescribed by my Psychiatrist, for severe depression. I take them exactly as prescribed.

After I take the Seroquel at bedtime, I start to feel tired about three hours later. When I turn out the light I am treated to amazing visuals until I fall asleep. Crazy geometric shapes in different colors move around in my vision. I just think of this as a positive side effect from the medications I need to take to be mentally stable.
