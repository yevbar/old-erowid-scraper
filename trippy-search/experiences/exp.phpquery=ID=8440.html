First and Last Time
DXM
by Mike

 
DOSE: 4 oz oral DXM (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


On a Saturday night, I decided to go buy some Robotussin and robo-trip. I sat down at 10 o'clock and took shots (the little cup they provide) with chasers of water. I have hated the taste of cough medicine since I was little and never used it to cure a cold. After the bottle was down I felt good, I played some computer games and while I did this my stomach felt a little uneasy. I figured I would be ok because I read that DXM makes users a little nauseous at the onset.

I got into my bed and lay there feeling sort of like I was stoned but with a bad stomach ache. It was the first time in about 4 years where I felt sick to my stomach and felt like I was going to puke (besides being drunk and puking etc). I went into the bathroom and put my head over the bowl for about 10 minutes and the puking sensations kept coming and going.

After I thought it was over with, I went back to my bead but decided to put a trash can near it in case I puked. 5 minutes later, I puked my guts out into the bag, after I was done I was sweating all over and just closed my eyes and went to sleep, I woke up 5 or 6 times during the night with the gross vomit taste in my mouth and went back to bed.

Finally I woke up at 8am to go to work. I felt ok except I was very hungry and when I drank a glass of water it felt very thick in my mouth. My taste sensations were WAY off the rest of the day and

I just started to feel better as I write this at 9pm after my day of work.

This experience definitely put me off of DXM.
