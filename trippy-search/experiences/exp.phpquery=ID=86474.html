by Adubz

 
DOSE: 20 mg oral Pharms - Escitalopram (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 255 lb

So I wouldn't kill myself, so I wouldn't feel sad. I stare in the mirror and see nothing. I think about sad things and my mind instantly sedates my emotions. That's why I haven't cried in months. That's why people think I'm uninterested in anything. Everyone thinks I'm strung out on drugs.
