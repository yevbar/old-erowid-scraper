mCPP, Amphetamines (Adderall XR) & Sleep Deprivation
by theboywithoutaname

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 40 mg oral Piperazines - mCPP
  T+ 2:36 60 mg oral Piperazines - mCPP
  T+ 2:36 30 mg oral Amphetamines

BODY WEIGHT: 220 lb


T-0M 40 MG mCPP Taken

T-38m

Not feeling much yet, only a little 'wierdness' but I can't put my finger on what it is.

T-58m

Kind of dizzyness set in, also very tired since I did not sleep last night. That might be attributing to this. I also feel some very mild nausea with a mouthwatering effect at this point. I do not feel any significant euphoric effects yet.

T-1h.32m

Feeling mild euphoria, tiredness still here, mild jaw clenching going on. Nausea and 'Mouth Watering effect' is completely gone now. I can say i'm at a +1 right now. Nothing major or magical yet, wondering if I took too little.

T-2h:36m

I'm probably at a +2 now, ingested 60mg more of mCPP and 30mg Adderall XR. Euphoria is good, seems like more a mellow euphoric bliss.

-Summary:

This chemical seems not to go so good on its own. After I added the Adderall + 60MG mCPP the euphoria went up around x5 fold. Still alot of exploring to do left on this chem. I don't think this will become a restricted chemical, since its abuse potential is probably less than a tylenol PM =P. It definitely does not live up to the hype.
