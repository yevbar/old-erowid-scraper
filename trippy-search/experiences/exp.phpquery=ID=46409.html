Most Amazing Pill Ever
Zolpidem
by Anonymous

 
DOSE: 3.0 mg oral Pharms - Zolpidem (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


I am an insomniac, and to add to my troubles, I stayed up partying till sunrise and slept all day. It was 4p.m. when I woke up and the next day I had to wake up for school at 6:30 in the morning. I usually try not to take sleeping aids, because it only makes my insomnia worse when I have to stop.

I had some free samples in my house, so I just decided to take one at 11:00, I laid in bed for about 20 minutes and I started thinking to myself, 'this will never work, I'm not tired at all.' Then like a switch I was out, the last thing I remember was thinking that last thought. I never even felt the sleepiness creeping up on me. When I woke up 5 minutes before my alarm, I felt like it was the weekend, I had never been so rested at 6:25 before in my life. I have heard that the psychoactive affects are very good when staying awake, but the sleeping affects are more than amazing.
