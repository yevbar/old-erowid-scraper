I Should Have Thought Twice
4-MTA
by Ice

 
DOSE:     4-MTA

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


[Erowid Note: This report is marked as a 4-MTA report, but readers should be aware there are aspects of it that suggest the identification is speculative.]

I'd rather be addicted to shooting heroin. I can say nothing good in retrospect about this drug. I woke up after 17 hours of sleeping, my nails are chewed to hell with dried blood and dirt and I think grease. I'm not exactly sure where my car is, and I have cuts and bruises all over my body and I dont know how I got them. I think I might never do drugs again because of this.

I came into contact with 4-mta about 6 days ago. I met a guy who I was buying chrystal from and he said his Friend who he refered to as 'chemist' (who does all the cooking) has been involved in cooking something new.

4-mta is one hell of a drug, it isnt even known too good on the streets by seasoned drug addicts or dealers. 99% of people I talked to about it never heard of it. I did it because someone has to be one of the first to try something. I know im not the first but im one of the few low percentage who was to try it.

I did it for the first time on saturday October 30th, 2004; its now wednesday November 3rd 2004 and I barely remember anything.
