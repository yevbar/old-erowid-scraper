The First Time Glow
AMT
by BBKF

 
DOSE: 30 mg oral AMT

BODY WEIGHT: 210 lb


Well, I finally got my AMT. A gram of fun, but what to start out with? It was a monday, and I decided due to time constraints and the fact that I would be around people not tripping (mainly my sister, who was also at home), that I would take a rather low dose of 30mg.

I'm working from home this summer, so I'd gotten a lot of sleep the night before and slept in till around 2pm after being up rather late the night before. This being said, I had also not eaten after waking up, as I wanted to avoid the nausea.

4:30pm: I put the AMT in a gelcap, and downed it with some water at about.

5:30: Stomach feeling a little funny, but nothing too bad. I wonder if maybe I'm just getting hungry after not eating for 12+ hours, so I have a hot pocket (sounded good to me) and it helps somewhat with the discomfort. Still not to much going on in my head, but I know its still early.

6:30: I decided to take a shower, and coming out I feel happy for pretty much no reason. This feeling would continue for the rest of the night.

7:00: Pupils are dilated pretty far. I hadn't really planned anything for the night, so I put on some music, got on irc and talked to a few friends. Although I didnt feel very affected by the drug, one or two of them asked if I had been drinking, (I'm a VERY happy drunk) so I guess my state had been altered.

7:45: Decide to take a walk to the store, it was still pretty nice out and I wanted to get something to drink. The walk to the store went by pretty quickly, but when I got to the store, I had a really hard time choosing what I wanted. I wandered around for about 30 minutes, and finally chose some Grape Soda, Jolly Ranchers, and Airheads. Yeah, really healthy heh.

8:15: Leave the store, and realize that if I was going to get any visuals, they would probably be showing up by now. Alas, it was not to be. Had a pleasant enough walk back home, and ended up just lounging around for the rest of the night.

4:00am: Decided I was tired, and went to bed listening to music and feeling pretty good.

Definitly a pleasant first experience, although I had hoped for some kind of mild visuals, and figured that it would be a little more intense. Feel fine the next day, almost as if I hadn't taken anything at all. I think this dosage would have been great to either dance with, or go to a party. I felt sociable and talked online quite a bit, wish it had been a weekend so I could have hung out with some friends. I'll be doing a 50mg dose next time, and I have high hopes for an even better experience.
