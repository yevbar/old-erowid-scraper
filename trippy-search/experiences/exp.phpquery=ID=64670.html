Smoking the Fly
Amanitas - A. muscaria
by Daytripper

 
DOSE: 1 bowl smoked Amanitas

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


Smoked 1 bowl full of material in a water pipe. About 5 hits total. Smoked only the flesh scraped from red skin.

Felt effects almost immediately. Very similar to marijuana. At first more focused, but as I type this 30 minutes later I'm a little dizzier and losing ability to focus.

Sounds are enhanced. Eyes are watering more than usual. Getting a little jumpy.

Overall, nice effect, seems to be a nice substitute for marijuana. Will definitely try again, probably blending with marijuana next time.
