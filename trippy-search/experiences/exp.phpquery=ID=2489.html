What an Experience
2C-T-7
by Lorenzo

 
DOSE: 35 mg insufflated 2C-T-7 (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


I finally got around to trying 2C-T-7. A friend of mine had always been on my back to try it, so a couple days ago I did. I loved it. Here's the itinerary for my night:

12:30am- I snort about 35mg of 2C-T-7. (snorting 2C-T-7 hurt my nose worse than any other drug- meth, crank, mdma, even AMT. And it hurts for like 15 or 30 minutes)

12:45am- Everything I look at waves around as if there are heat waves being emitted from the floor. My nose still hurts.

1am- Nose stops hurting. I feel like I'm on the best MDMA I've ever had and the hallucinations are very profound. Rotating 3-D octagon shapes are inside every object I look at. Very overwhelming.

1am-3am- Feeling and hallucinations continue, feeling tapers away and is gone by 3:15am. The hallucinations continue. I am finally able to leave the house.

3:30am- Go to a park and play for an hour or so. My hallucinataions taper down slowly and I am done by around 9am. I could have easily fallen asleep at around five, though.

So thats how it went. I suggest this drug to anyone expierienced with acid, definately not a drug for a first time psychedelic user.
