Good but Frustrating
4-Methylmethcathinone
by non3

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 50 mg insufflated 4-Methylmethcathinone (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:30 50 mg insufflated 4-Methylmethcathinone (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:50 100 mg oral 4-Methylmethcathinone (liquid)
  T+ 2:30   repeated smoked Cannabis  

BODY WEIGHT: 72 kg


Yesterday I tried out a batch of mephedrone (4-methylmethcathinone) for the first time. The powder has a consistency of flaky crystals that are very light. The powder is of pure white colour and without odor. The taste is very bitter and chemical. I have seen a batch of a friend of mine that was very smelly and was off white to yellowish. Doses of 200 mg were measured on a precision scale.

13.00 I split the first 200 mg package in four lines of 50 mg and insufflated a line. The burning sensation in my nose was very severe.

13.10 I definitely feel some amphetamine-like effects but very subtle and without any physical signs. There was a tingling sensation as I get with all stimulants.

13.30 I insufflate another 50 mg line. This time the burning sensation is even worse and my eyes start to get watery. I will not insufflate this ever again.

13.50 Feeling quite good but strange. There is definitely amphetamine like effects but there is also something else – a heavy head and mild euphoria. My heart rate by this time was 100 bpm. As the effects were not very strong I decided to take another 100 mg dissolved in orange juice.

14.20 Definitely feeling high, there is quite a lot of euphoria but there are no empathogenic effects from this chemical. For me the feeling would be best described as the coming down from MDMA, when I still feel good but am not loved up. Music sounded really good and I was happy to listen to anything without vocals. My heart beat rate was 120 bpm.

15.00 I had a feeling that I still didn’t have a breakthrough with this chemical and decided to take another 100 mg in OJ.

15.30 By this time I felt quite euphoric but very empty. I didn’t have any sexual desire as I do with amphetamines and MD(M)A. My heart rate was 120 – 130 bpm. My pupils were normal (I think). I am deciding if I should take another 100 mg but decide not to as I didn’t get the effects that I like. I smoke a joint and feel much better.

16.30 The peak is slowly getting over and I start to see some strange visuals. I start to see things appearing in the corner of my eye but when I look at them they disappear. This happens to me when I am on a lot of amphetamines.

17.30 The effects are mostly gone but I still have a full head and a strange feeling. The comedown isn’t really problematic and there is no crankiness.

At 19.00 I feel quite normal and am fully functional again. Sleep was quite light and I kept waking up the whole night but I am quite sensitive to stimulants so this wasn’t a surprise.

All in all this is an interesting compound but it is quite mild. I would not be comfortable doing anything more than 300-400 mg but at that dosage the effects are just not worth it. If you like euphoria then this may be worthwhile, but for me this compound lacks something.
