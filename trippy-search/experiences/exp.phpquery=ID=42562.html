Alpha and Omega
Mushrooms (P. tampanensis)
by J. Freeman

 
DOSE: 2.5 g oral Mushrooms - P. tampanensis (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I accepted Him into my world; He showed me the path He made.
He and I chose to kill me, but not before an hour of play.
My mind wouldn't surrender, so it was slayed. My soul washed away.
And then, as I learned how everything really is, I realize I am God.
'I am the alpha *and* the omega!' I remembered I did once say.
And there We were, staring at each other in dismay,
thinking, 'So this is how it all ends?
I *never* would have guessed (what We had made)!'
