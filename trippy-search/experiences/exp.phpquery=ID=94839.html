First Notable Bad Trip
Methoxetamine
by xET

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 50 mg insufflated Methoxetamine (powder / crystals)
  T+ 7:00 70 mg insufflated Methoxetamine (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 80 kg


I insufflated exactly 50mg at around 1pm, and tripped regularly as I normally would, as I use MXE a lot. Then later on, about 8pm I had another line of 70 mg. Instead of all the normal signs of tripping, I felt numb and cold, with no euphoria or happiness, just tingly all over and it wasn't pleasant.

This 'bad trip' lasted about 2 hours and the effects began to wear off. This was the first noteable bad trip from MXE I've ever had, and I've been using it for about 7 months.
