Hearing Voices
diphenhydramine (Benadryl), Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine) & DXM
by Bryan

 
DOSE:   repeated   Diphenhydramine
    repeated   Dimenhydrinate
    repeated   DXM

BODY WEIGHT: 145 lb


I abused diphenhydramine and dimenhydrinate on a regular basis w/ and without DXM for about 1-1/2 to 2 years. Ever since then I hear voices every day, the same way I hear them when I am on a dramamine trip. I recommend to never abuse either of these substances because hearing voices every day sucks.
