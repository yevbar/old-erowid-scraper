Lasting Spot in My Vision
5-MeO-DiPT
by Anonymous

 
DOSE:   oral 5-MeO-DiPT (powder / crystals)


I'm writing you to let you know of a potential side effect from high doses of 5-Meo-DiPT taken every weekend for 6 weeks.

After taking 30mg every weekend for 5 weeks, I dose around 40mg in the evening. After the trip I noticed some halucinations occuring in my vision. Every now and then a spot would pop up in my right eyes field of vision. Now I did put my finger on my eye before I fell asleep and was having halucinations.

But every now and then this Spot will pop up and I'll be like trying to make it go away.
