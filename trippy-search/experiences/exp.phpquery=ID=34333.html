Understanding the Medicinal Pleasure
Methadone
by Sandy Beaches

 
DOSE: 40 mg oral Methadone (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 115 lb


I have been taking 20 mgs of Methadone twice a day for the past four years.

I have experienced no side effects, no 'high,' no 'low,' no anything except pain relief.

I have two other family members who take Methadone for pain, and neither have any side effects or 'highs.'

We all receive our Methadone via prescriptions.

I have found Methadone to be a safe effective pain reliever with no side effects.
