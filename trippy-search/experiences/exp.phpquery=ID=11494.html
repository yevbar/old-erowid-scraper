A Match Made in Heaven
AMT & MDMA (Ecstasy)
by 5L-Jubeii

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 7.0 mg oral AMT (powder / crystals)
  T+ 2:00 0.5 tablets insufflated MDMA (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb


I was not prepared for a 48-hour weekend. It was only supposed to be a Friday-night get-together. I only went because ther was supposed to be some high-grade bud there, but it only ended up being really harsh gel-tabs. I dind't want to stay because I didn't like the acid. I had taken some the weekend before, and decided that it was far too strong for me. I was going to leave when the owner approached me with some great new and legal substance. He sold me on the idea and we went over to his dealer's place.

I wasn't too sure about it. After a lil' bit of bickering they decided that I should have as much as I wanted for free that night; they called it my moving-away gift as I would leave the following Thursday. I only asked for one dose as I am very careful of what chemicals I put in my body. Especially new ones.

We got back to the party and it was going strong. After 2 hours I was getting pretty pissed because nothing was happening. So i split a roll with a friend and we went and snorted it. The roll came pretty quick and it seemed like it would be the kind where you just want to sit and chill. I wasn't expecting it to last too long and it started to die about 2 hour later (4 hours after AMT dose). I was getting thirsty and I decided to get something to drink.

I don't think I ever made it to a full standing position. As soon as my rear left the usually hard and uncomfortabel surface of the couch, I felt my senses being sucked into the back of my head. I thought I was going to black-out; but as soon as I hit the sofa, I felt like I was in Utopia. The couch and I just clicked. I was in love with it. I didn't want to move, and I achieved this for about an hour.

Then I became very jumpy. I went and busted into every conversation I could find. I even had an intelligent conversation with the dog. At this point I was on a very peaceful roll. I was very calm of thought, but I wanted to be absolutely everywhere at once and with everyone. My vision was out the door. It was beyond that. I couldn't focus on anything. Everything was moving, breathing, thinking. My senses were maxed out, and I didnt miss a thing. I heard and saw every detail of the back room while I wasn't even ther. But I didn't comprehend a thing.

About 10 hours after I dosed AMT I started to come down from my roll. I wanted to lay down. I found the floor, the closest thing. I laid there in front of the TV watching Fraggle Rock. I didn't comprehend or make sense of it at all. It scared me. I started going into these pleasurable little convulsions which turned out to be shivers. For the first time that night, and day, I noticed how sweaty and clammy I was. I got a blanket and felt myself melt into the couch.

Most of the people were going now or crashing where they were. A few of us decided to watch Anime Videos. I recommend to everyone that Anime be a big part of your AMT experience. It was great. We watched TV until nightfall. I felt as if I knew each character personally. I remained like this until the next morning, Sunday. It was 36 hours after I dosed AMT.

I was up and running until about 5 P.M. that day. I got tired really quick and had a friend take me home. I went and crashed. Hard. I was out for 16 hours; just in time for work. It was an easy day and all I could think of was finding this new and wonderful substance at the place I was moving to.

I definately enjoyed this experince.
