Prebagalin (Lyrica)

DOSE: 450 mg oral Pharms - Pregabalin (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 172 lb

I Decided tonight to take a higher dose than I normally do to see what effect it might have on me. So far I am very pleased. It produces an effect similar to a high dose of valium. Kind of like the first time I ever tried a Benzo. My moter function is completly lost as I wander around with a cloud like feeling of floating. Things seem to be a lot more funny to me than when not taking the drug, like I'm giggling at everything for no reason. It's quite fun.
