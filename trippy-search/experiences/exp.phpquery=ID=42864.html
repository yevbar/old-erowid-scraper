Drug Testing & Opiates
by Nyte

 
DOSE:   IV Opiates

BODY WEIGHT: 190 lb


I was surprised by a Laboratory urine screen in the situation I was in; I Had just used opiates about 8 hours before the test.

I figured it would defiantly be positive. But I Drank a bunch of water (nearly a gallon) ASAP and urinated twice before testing I also made sure I used a sample from the middle of the urination. I don’t know for sure if that helps at all but I had heard that, and needed all the help I could get so I did it.

The test was sent in and came back negative in about a week of the test date.
