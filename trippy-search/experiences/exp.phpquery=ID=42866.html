What a Head Rush
Clonidine
by Myleshigh

 
DOSE: 0.8 mg oral Pharms - Clonidine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb

I usually take about 1-2 but decided to take 8. It was a very awesome experience. I was laying in my bed when all of a sudden it felt like I was sailing on the high seas. Up and down I went for quite some. Also I got very light headed this lasted for who knows how long until I finally passed out. as I woke up I felt fine but when I stood up I got the biggest head rush of my life.
