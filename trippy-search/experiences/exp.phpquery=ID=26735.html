Salvia divinorum
by Kyle

 
DOSE: 2 hits smoked Salvia divinorum

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


I heard about salvia from on of my friends and was quite eager to try it after hearing about it. So as any other curious mind is I decided to try it.

We were sitting in my buddies car I was in the front seat and my bud was in the backseat and my other friend was driving. We packed the first bowl and I was the first to hit it so it did, as soon as i exhaled the smoke I was quite unaware of what hit me, It felt almost like a big oval shape toilet paper roll covered in smooth spikes was molding my body into an L shape. I said man, this is crazy gimme another hit, so I took another one.

I immediately lost consciousness and an insanely strong force pulled me somewhere else. I felt like I was sitting in a whole bunch of ice cream and the window that I was leaning against was a big sheet of ice cream splitting my head in half. As I looked on one side of the plate of ice cream it was this playskool, plastic-like world where this lady and her little boy were pushing a stroller up and down the street over and over. I could figure out what was going on. At the same time I could hear my buddies laughing at me in the background and for some reason it made qiute angry and really really scared.

All of a sudden I came to and was like what the hell just happened, I could figure it out. I was feeling an incredible feeling, almost as if i had pins and needles all over my body but it was 1000 times worse than I had ever had them, and that were little hands pulling at the skin on my back. This feeling went on for about half an hour. I finally came back to full reality and could figure out what just happened, to me it was something absolutley insane and out of this world I have never seen anything like and never will again, unless on salvia.

After trying it a gew more times I became extremely scared and worried and thought everyone was out to get me while I was high. Needless to say I refrained from using it again.
