5-MeO Beta Testing
5-MeO-DMT & Cannabis
by Visation

 
DOSE: 5.0 mg   5-MeO-DMT (powder / crystals)
    smoked Cannabis (flowers)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


I am writing here to describe my first experience with 5 Meo DMT, which happened last night. After spending many hours researching on this site, I decided that 5 Meo DMT was the tryptamine I wanted to try. My experience with entheogenic substances is not that extensive: A couple of mushroom trips (p.cubensis and amanitas muscaria), one rather unremarkable experience with morning glory seeds, some failed salvia attempts and a whole lot of chronic.

Needless to say, I was a little hesitant when I opened up the package in the mail containing a small plastic envelope with 250mg of 5meo in it. However, curiosity soon got the best of me as I sprinkled about 5 mg onto the bowl I was about to smoke. The taste was hardly offensive (plastic, but a good clean kind of plastic) and the smoke was a little harsher than normal. I definitely noticed a strong body buzz and a tripping headspace that was somewhere between 'whoa! I'm really stoned' and 'Damn, these mushrooms are the shit!' however I must say it was nowhere near the intensity of most of the experience reports I've read on this site. That's probably because I only did a small amount.

Later that evening I dipped a finger into the bag and picked up a couple of grains (about 5mg again by my estimation - could've been less), which I promptly insufflated. This experience was a little more intense, although it took longer to come on. I had a warm body buzz and very unfocused cognition. It was quite a task for me to find the remote to my television, let alone brush my teeth so I could go to bed. This lasted for about 2 hours until I was finally able to fall asleep.

That was last night. Today I am a little groggy (due to lack of sleep more than anything else) and occasionally have little mini-flashbacks, although nothing serious.

All in all, I would say it was a fun experience, although nothing too out of control. Most definitely something worth trying again when I am able to get the full effects, and I will be able to make a better judgement.
