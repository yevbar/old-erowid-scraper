Loving Kindness
Meditation & Nitrous Oxide
by The Odeol

 
DOSE:   inhaled Nitrous Oxide

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


After 15 minutes of loving meditation
I inhaled nitrous
The patterns behind my eyelids formed sublime
The term mountainous love repeated
A glimpse of pure love
We wear the world like clothes
Must be spiritual nudists
(buddhists)
Falling over in conversations
over words
(sublime)
