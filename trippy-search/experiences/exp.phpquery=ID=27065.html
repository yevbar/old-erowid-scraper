Nutmeg Not Worth It
Nutmeg
by Tryp

 
DOSE: 20 g oral Nutmeg (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 110 lb


I had haerd about the pschoactive effects of nutmeg and decided to try it out. I'd previously tried smoking and drinking a tea from it with no effect so decided to try eating it. This is what happened.

7am - 20g of whole nutmeg ground into powder and drunk with OJ

8am - feeling nausious, and decided to go back to sleep.

4pm - woke up feeling sick and a little dizzy

4:30 - a lot more dizzy, alot more sick and threw up

5pm - a little less sick and extremely dizzy, slight visual distortions, same for next hour

6pm - pass out on bed

11:30 next morning - woke up, still feeling quite dizzy but not sick

1pm - effects wearing off

3pm - back to baseline

Overall I must say I did not enjoy the experience, will not try it again, and do not reccommend any one else trys it.
