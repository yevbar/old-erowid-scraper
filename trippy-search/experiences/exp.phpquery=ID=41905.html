Knockout
Salvia divinorum (10x)
by Be fearful of gonzo

 
DOSE: 1.0 g insufflated Anadenanthera colubrina (ground / crushed)
  17 seeds   H.B. Woodrose (ground / crushed)
  0.3 g smoked Salvia divinorum (extract - 10x)
  2.0 mg oral Pharms - Alprazolam (pill / tablet)
    smoked Cannabis (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb



I cant remember most of it I kinda blacked out for a minute but what I do remember is its fucked up, it’s unexplainable; it was great until I took a huge toke of salvia and it just knocked me on my ass like a someone hit me in the face with a weapon with mind destroying power. I couldn’t understand anything anyone was saying but I thought I understood. I thought it was over for my mind at the time during this hit then I calmed down and realized it was just the drugs. Then I felt great for another 6 hours then I blacked out and woke up around noon and smoked more salvia with friends. She got pissed off at me cause the high wasn’t what she was expecting, she said it felt like she was falling off a cliff. I describe my trip as a acid frenzy with mellow cool waves. But damn that salvia.

I had great sense of nirvana and floating up and falling down again and again time and space seemed to be at my finger tips but didn’t seem to amazed as I was so many times before but hey its been 17 hours since and I’m still felling the seeds: but most vision where at my control such as colors red blue = neon of every
