Lovely Discovery
Products - Other Chemicals ('Stain Remover', speculated to be 4-Methylmethcathinone)
by Toad

 
DOSE: 250 mg oral Products - Bath Salts, Plant Food, etc (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 195 lb


I do not know exactly what is in the legally obtained packet that I have now sampled on 4 different occasions. That’s a little frustrating, that in this nudge-nudge-wink-wink fringe legality that we are currently dealing with, the information available on the products is limited—one thing this isn’t is what it is labeled as, which is stain remover. Anyway, broadly speaking, it is Meow. It is an absolutely wonderful addition to my life, and I will be using it regularly.

I have insufflated it and I have mixed it with water and drunk it. I will discuss drinking it (250 mg of the particular brand I have been using mixed with cup or so of water, down it all at once), which gives what I think is a stronger high, and it also gives me a moment of fairly strong nausea about a half-hour after consumption. I haven’t yet actually succumbed and puked, but it’s something I’m coming to realize is part of the deal if I drink this stuff. No big deal—I’m actually already high by then and feeling like all is right, so I just take this nausea in stride, and it passes pretty quickly. Still—I won’t drink it on an empty stomach. The other physiological reactions I note are sweating pretty profusely during the initial rush, and a bit of heart racing.

It comes on strong and fast—15 minutes after drinking it, I’m feeling it, 20 minutes is stronger still, and it continues to increase up to about 40 minutes after consumption, when it levels off for a great, ecstasy-like ride that lasts until about two hours after consumption. I don’t note the same abrupt comedown that others do, it fades out nicely for me. I am able to resist any urge to redose—or give in to that urge, as the situation calls for.

Among the features of the high for me—keeping in mind, this is drinking it mixed with water, and I do think that produces a different high than insufflating—are: A rather interesting time distortion during the first rush—moments linger as time slows down. A strong mood elevation—life is good, and this reminds me that I know that. I want to tell the people I love that I really do love them. Music, which can be the foreground of many highs for this music lover, fades to the background while I contemplate life and love and friendship. There are no visual or aural hallucinations, and this is not a psychedelic experience, it’s a bliss thing.

I don’t lose touch with reality, but am rather overwhelmed by reality’s silver lining.

As I write this, I have obtained this legally, in a perfectly above-board commercial transaction, using a credit card. I want this situation to continue [...] This stuff is pretty strong.
