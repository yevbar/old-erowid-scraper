Happy Trails
Paroxetine
by Rock

 
DOSE: 20 mg oral Pharms - Paroxetine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 130 lb


I just read some of the reports about Paxil. I thought it was just me that had the bad withdrawal symptoms and saw trails in the morning. I wonder sometimes why I only see traisl when I wake up. Even if I nap during the day I wake up and see them. Also, when I first started taking it, I ran out after about 2 months, and I was a wreck!! I started acting like I was 5 or something. I threw tantrums, I cried at the drop of a hat, I was all clingy with my grrrlfriend. It was a terrible time. I'm thinkin that maybe I will switch drugs. Paxil has been more trouble than it is worth
