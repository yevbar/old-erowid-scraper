At Its Best
Hydrocodone with Acetaminophen (Vicodin/Lortab), Hydroxyzine (Atarax), Amitriptyline (Elavil) & Ondansetron (Zofran)
by Greenboy?

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 22.5 mg oral Hydrocodone (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:00 2250 mg oral Acetaminophen (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:50 75 mg oral Pharms - Hydroxyzine (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:50 25 mg oral Pharms - Amitriptyline (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:50 8 mg oral Pharmaceuticals (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb


Info: I've used my share of drugs like opiates, from codeine (very weak), to morphine, heroin, & Fentanyl I.V. (very strong). Depressants like valium, ativan, and xanax. I've also used soma a muscle relaxer, weed, cocaine, ketamine, and XTC or “E” before. I'm very healthy and I’ve never had any problems with addiction. I’m a very experienced drug user. I’ve used drugs since my early teens.

Okay so I fractured my right arm a few days ago, and I’m in severe pain so I go to the E.R., it took 5 hours to just get a room. Finally the doctor did some exams (x-rays) and prescribes some 5mg-500mg hydrocodone for moderate to severe pain. She told me to take 1 to 2 tabs or 5mg to 10mg & 500mg-1000mg of Tylenol. I had some Tylenol with codeine lying around and used that instead for pain and saved the hydrocodone for later, man did I make a good decision!

Remember I was using codeine for a week straight before this so I probably developed a tolerance to it; codeine and hydrocodone are very similar substances. Okay I decide not to eat for 8 hours and not to take any pills. I grabbed 4 1/2 hydrocodone pills and break them in halves and swallow it with 3 ounces of water, which makes the effects last longer and it makes it easier on the stomach.

Time: 5-12mins: I start to feel relaxed and a little dizzy.

Time: 12-25mins: I started to feel more relaxed, dizzy, and have a very, very mild nausea. I felt great, mild euphoria and lightheadedness.

Time: 25-50mins: Moderate Euphoria, very relaxed, lightheadedness, and I started to talk to my dad, which I never do. I felt like if I loved everybody felt a good, strong high and felt that I was on top of the world. I though that it was about to peak and I was right, and I didn’t want this to stop, it felt GREAT!

Time: 50-75mins: It has peaked!!! Man I felt awesome, Strong euphoria or high, now I’m kind of itchy and I felt like if I breathe less but nothing major. It felt like a heroin high with the xtc “hug everyone” and morphine’s since of well being and pot’s relaxing effect. I took some atarax 75mg for the itching because it was getting a little worse, some amitriptyline 25mg for anxiety, that way the high will last a bit longer and some zofran 8mg because I didn’t want to puke while my parents were around.

Time: 75-100mins: I was still high the itching got a bit better. I still felt some nausea but I didn’t puke. I felt great right now just those small discomforts. I went to my room and put on some relaxing music, it lets me enjoy the high better. I felt like chatting but I just wanted to stay were I was and enjoy everything.

Time: 100-150mins: I’m starting to sweat and get dizzier my high is coming down; it was not as strong as before but still potent and very enjoyable. My itching finally stopped and I no longer felt nauseous. I still felt great just a little sleepier than before, probably the atarax & the amitriptyline made me sleepier. I felt like if I loved everyone.

Time 150-200mins: The high was still there, but now its moderate and I still felt sleepy. I still felt dizzy so I lied down. Now instead of feeling high I felt more relaxed than high.

Time 200-250mins: The high is hardly there, what a bomber I’m starting to feel a little itchy again I think the dizziness and the lightheadedness stopped, but the relaxed feeling won’t go away until tomorrow morning when I take a shower.

Time: 250-300mins: The high, dizziness, lightheadedness, nausea, itching, are all gone the only thing left was a little relaxed… It was a great experience not the best, but a good one. I think I could’ve had a better one if I hadn’t taken the codeine for a straight week before.

Comparisons: Hydrocodone’s high is like a mix of heroin, “E”, morphine and weed. Like a heroin’s strong high, “E’s” love everyone sensation, morphine’s well being, and pot’s relaxation and lightheadedness.
