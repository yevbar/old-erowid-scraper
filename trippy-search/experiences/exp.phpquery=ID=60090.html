More Than I Bargained For
IAP
by Harley

 
DOSE: 200 mg oral IAP

BODY WEIGHT: 250 lb


A very long and high plateau was reached after about an hour and a half of a fairly strong push forward. It is quite reminiscent of my early experiments with low doses of LSD. There are marked difference though. It is not nearly as introspective, a bit more energetic, and there is a slight loss of equilibrium. A novel compound in and of itself, for sure.
