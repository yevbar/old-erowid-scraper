Triazolam (Halcion) & Alcohol
by Ajeezy

 
DOSE: 1 mg oral Pharms - Triazolam (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb

Then we got a bottle of jack (all of that I didn't remember). Me and his continued to drink my memory of that is shady from the first shot, but after the bottle was gone, I remember trying to gather my stuff. I was falling all over the place with a memory that was blacking out. I asked to spend the night I was so tired and had no coordination. I can't remember walking home but I did succesfully. The halcion killed my memory and made me very loopy despite my very high benzo tolerence. Halcion should only be used for sleep I never took outside my house again. It's very strong short acting benzo use as prescribed.
