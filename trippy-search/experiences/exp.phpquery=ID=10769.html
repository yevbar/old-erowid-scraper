Kanna-Good To Smoke On
Sceletium Tortuosum
by PuristLove

 
DOSE: 250 mg smoked Sceletium tortuosum (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


I had done a little reading about this wonderful herb on the internet but found no information about it here on erowid. That surprised me as this is probably my favorite resource.

I am still high on Sceletium as I type this. Its been about half an hour since I finished smoking. This is a pretty subtle plant, comparable to a mild pot high, with a bit more euphoria and a bit less body high. I have yet to try ingesting it orally, I'll save that experiment for next week. This is a good recreational substance, and would be great for relaxing after a long day or hanging out with friends. Not much more than that, but definitely fun.
