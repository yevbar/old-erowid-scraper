Quantum Multiverse
5-MeO-DMT & Salvia divinorum (10x extract)
by JMT

 
DOSE:   smoked Salvia divinorum (extract - 10x)
  5.0 mg smoked 5-MeO-DMT (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


My experiences with Salvia have been mostly uneventfull, usually I become prickly, sweaty, and irritated with a subtle but bizarre shift in consciousness that I don't realize has occurred. Weird stuff to be certain...

Anyhow a very small amount of Salvia 10x extract was placed in a glass pipe, and perhaps 5mg of 5-MeO-DMT was sprinkled atop it. It was smoked in one hit(which turned out to be almost a lungful) and held in for maybe 30 seconds.

I was swiftly vaulted into the most intensly Cosmic space I've yet experienced. It felt like I had entered something REALLY big and REALLY universal. Intense, folding geometry was all that was was of me, and what I saw and I felt I was no longer me but a part of this 'matrix' or whatever it was. I cannot emphasize enough how intensly weird, alien, and utterly cosmic this felt- like I'd broken some type of rule or had entered ENTIRELY different universes of some type. I say 'universes' as it seemed everything was splitting of in all directions at the same time, if that makes any sense. Also, it felt oddly familiar, or that it was a place that was always there and was 'what's really going on' or some such thing...

I was frightened of course but was so wacked that I hadn't the time to dwell on this fear or allow it to encompass me. In laymans terms, I was 'completely fucked-up', and by the time I had gotten some sense of bearing and concern about whether or not I'd 'survive' or return to 'normal', I was coming down.

As soon as I gained a sense of my surroundings and my place in them, I leapt from my bed and paced about the house, amidst intense visual and cosmic activity. Everything was 'fractalized', intensly psychedelic, and looked *EXTREMELY* futuristic and otherwise astonishing. I felt I was moving from place to place by will of my 'mind' or 'being' and had no recollection of using my body. As though I was moving in small quantum jumps, although I was in no way enjoying or noticing it at the time- I was simply reacting.

Felt like an atom or quark or something zipping around amongst similar such things, a part of the universe described by physics, not the one we humans move about in/experience/have knowledge of in our day to day lives.

I came down very slowly, I assume due to the longer duration of the Salvia, and was in a diminished/ing form of the state for a good hour. Physically I was uncomfortable and mildly nauseous and was otherwise eager to be free of the effects.

In summary the experience was neither that enjoyable or that unenjoyable - it was just very very intense and astonishing. If I had ANY idea that the effects would be so intense maybe I'd could have expected it and enjoyed it more. Was quite excited and otherwise giddy for a good bit after I came down - that 'holy shit!' type reaction...

Anyhow, quite a ride, and in the future I'm interested in trying the same combo but perhaps using a bit less of of both ;-)
