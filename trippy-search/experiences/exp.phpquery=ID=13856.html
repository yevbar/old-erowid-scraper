The Power Of Grapefruit Juice!
dextromethorphan (DXM)
by DXMTRIPPER

 
DOSE: 236 mg oral DXM (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


I have heard that grapefruit juice has an impact on the DXM experience and I just recently got a chance to test it out. It apparantly makes the DXM experience a lot stronger and longer in duration.

I use DXM pretty heavily and it has been a while since 1 bottle of Vicks 44 (cough only) will give me a real good buzz. I usually dose between 500 and 1000 mg. And that is all through cough syrup I drink. I don't have access to the pure DXM powder.

Anyway, I drunk about a liter of grapefruit juice an hour or so before I took only 1 bottle of Vicks 44 (cough only), 236 mg of DXM. And expected to get only slightly buzzed since I am used to much higher doses. Well, I could feel myself coming up and I knew that I would get a much better buzz when I felt myself enter the 2nd plateau (see the DXM FAQ). My buzz just kept getting stronger and stronger and the experience lasted probably a half an hour longer.

I would recommend this to anyone who uses DXM because it made me trip like the first time I had ever taken DXM which is really fun, for most people anyway. I think next time I am going to try more DXM probably in the 500 mg range and the grapefruit juice. Well, that is all I have to say for now. Take care everyone and stay safe.
