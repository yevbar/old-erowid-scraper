Tobacco
by xoing mili

 
DOSE:   smoked Tobacco


I live in california, the state leading the charge against tobacco. It's odd that in a place known for people doing their own thing, that a powerful group is trying to hassle others. Anyway, i've been a cigarette smoker for 25 years. When the anti-cigarette gang started ramming their agenda down our throats, I started to experiment with other forms of tobacco, just to piss them off.

Well, there were other reasons too. My best discovery was the cigar. Now, I know what you're thinking. I've tried those gas station cigars in the past, and nearly became sick. But a fine cigar is a whole other story. The taste is a hell of a lot better than a cigarette. Cigarettes taste like garbage to me now. And, the effect is different. A cigarette delivers a quick hit of nicotine. Once I get my fix, smoking loses its pleasure. I just finish the cigarette because I already lit it. Cigars deliver nicotine slowly. It takes half an hour to get the nicotine fix. With each puff during that time, I enjoy getting just a little bit more nicotine, until eventually I am satisfied. I would love to get one of ahnold's special gubinatorial cigars :)
