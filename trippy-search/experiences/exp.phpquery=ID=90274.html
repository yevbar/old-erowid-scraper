Perfect Cocktail
Piracetam, 4-Fluoroamphetamine & Methylone
by Tiberius

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 1.5 g oral Piracetam
  T+ 3:00 150 mg oral 4-Fluoroamphetamine
  T+ 5:00 200 mg oral Methylone
  T+ 8:00 1 cig. smoked Cannabis

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


0:00 1.5 grams Piracetam
3:00 150 mg 4-FA
5:00 200 mg Methylone
8:00 One joint Cannabis

A friend recently bought piracetam, which I promptly looked up. There were a few reports that state that using Piracetam before Methylone really lengthened the plateau, from an average of 1.5 to two hours to more like 4-5 hours. Another interesting reported effect was that piracetam mellows the comedown.

The Experience:

Mindset: Excited for electronic show that was happening later that night.
I had already planned to take 4-fa and then Methylone later, because of the synergy between the two substances. 2 hours before the electronic show started, I took the 1.5 grams of Piracetam, and felt no mental effects from it.

Arrived at show, and started taking photos ( my job that night). Took the 4-fa 30 minutes before the show was going to start. People crowded the dance floor and the lights were very well done. I could not stop moving, but I was not rushed like many of the amphetamines that I have experienced. The 4-fa enhanced my communication ability, and I was less inhibited. This lead to me meeting and exchanging information with two of the performers that night. There is never any pupil dilation that I have found with 4-fa (my experiences have always been below 180 mg).

Two hours into the show, it seems like a good time to take the methylone. In retrospect I probably should have dosed less. The comeup was dizzying, very MDMA like, and very unlike my previous methylone experience. I started to overheat for a little bit, and worry about what I was doing to my body, and question why I took another substance when was feeling dandy just on the 4-fa. But I got my thoughts in control, and the overheating stopped. Pupils were extremely dilated.

My thoughts were very calm, and not all over the place. I felt that I was suspended in my own personal water tank that I could bring around with me, so weightless and light I felt. Sound was crisp- a few of my friends were already losing some hearing due to the loud show, but I could hear them everytime. It was harder to start dancing again, but when I got going, it brought back memories of my first and second MDMA experience. I felt that the crowd and the music became serene, and my mind was totally into the moment, thinking about the future was almost impossible. A girl motioned to talk to me, and she pressed her hand against my cheek as she whispered into my ear- Her touch shocked my being to its core- I never knew that touch could be such an electric, mind blowing experience.

The show was over at Midnight. Luckily there was an after party to go to, for I was full of a calm energy. Contrary to many of the experience reports concerning Cannabis usage and Methylone, I found the pairing to be very good. It was as if the Cannabis was adding Color back into my world that was whited out by the ecstasy I experienced.
Ran into a friend who was disappointed by his bunk MDMA. Told him of my experience and he as very much interested in trying the combination.

Conclusions:

Piraceam + Methylone = Return of Magic?

There is definitely something special with piracetam/methylone combinations. Mine is not the first report to tell of how great methylone can be when used with piracetam. I found the 4-fa to be a great intro, since it provides long lasting subtle stimulation without being jittery, at least in my case. Usually I end up with a comedown much later, the same with methylone. However, I had no problems going to sleep after the afterparty. Which is pretty amazing to me considering the amount of stimulants I consumed that night.
