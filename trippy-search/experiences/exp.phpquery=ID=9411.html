Cat trip
Catnip
by mittenman

 
DOSE: 5 bowls smoked Catnip (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


Last week four friends and I were in a local store when one buddy brought up the fact that he had read that catnip could get you high. Whatever, we thought, it was worth a try for a price of $2.39 per half ounce (don't we all wish weed costed this much?). So we bought the bag and walked to a nearby park where the four of us sat in a circle and my friend W proceeded to pack a fat bowl of the stuff. We passed the bowl around and were surprised that the burning catnip smelled almost reminiscent to pot. The smoke had a more 'sharp' feeling to it than marijuana and also had a bit of an unsettling flavour. After smoking 5 bowls we stood up and began to walk home. Upon standing I could definately notice a marijuana-like sedated buzz, but it was almost too weak to identify. The motions of my eyes seemed to be a bit behind the motions of my head, my limbs felt slightly heavy and all-in-all I felt quite relaxed. Upon returning home I noticed that I felt even more docile, like all I wanted to do was relax.

The catnip high was far from strong, but it was definately worth trying and costed almost nothing.
