Bloody Blood Blood
Melatonin & Coffee
by Anonymous

 
DOSE:   repeated oral Melatonin (pill / tablet)
    repeated oral Coffee (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 130 lb


I recently obtained a bottle of Naturalist Melatonin (3 mg/cap, includes 89mg Calcium, 69mg Phosphorus). I find myself frequently lying in bed for several hours with a very racing, creative mind, which is good in some ways, but bad in others (obviously lacking sleeping time). I had heard/read some good things about Melatonin and I decided it would be a good choice for a sleep aide.

Wrong, so wrong. Well, at least for my case. The first time I took it, I noticed it's effects come quickly. It was kind of interesting. I noticed euphoria, with a sense that, 'God I need to be in bed so badly', while colors seemed to appeal a little bit more, and things kind of had a stoned effect. Lying in bed I had a bit of an unpleasant feeling, can't describe it too well.

I took Melatonin three days in a row (the bottle simply says WARNING: Do not take more than 1 tablet within 24 hours, each time was ~24hrs of the last). I also drank Coffee in the morning 2 of the 3 days as well, which is something definitely to be noted. On the third day, while I was in class, I suddenly felt like water was coming out of my nose. Random nose bleed, first time I have ever had one, second time my nose has ever bled. After thinking for a bit, I concluded it must have been the Melatonin, and Googled it. I found some sites referring to blood thinning/minor bleeding as a result from Melatonin, so I am pretty sure that Melatonin is involved.

Yesterday I took Melatonin again (about a week without using Melatonin at all), because I had been falling asleep fairly late, and I needed to wake up at 5:30 AM this morning because I was going fishing. It worked as it had before, and I fell asleep within about 45 minutes. I was feeling unpleasant as hell falling asleep though, especially because of the bad memory of my previous nosebleed. I would have slight pains in my stomach, individual fingers might get sudden bursts of pain, and overall I felt very uncomfortable. This persisted throughout the next day. Then later in the day I went to the bathroom and had bloody stool.

I realize now that taking Melatonin with Caffeine is a bad idea anyways, and why I took it before researching is stupid. Regardless, I found Melatonin to be a little unpleasant and contrary to what I have heard, Melatonin produced blank, black sleep for me, with absolutely no dreaming/recall.

Good thing I test-drove this sleep aide before using it incase of a bad Shroom trip. It doesn't even make me fall asleep anyways, just gives me that tired sensation that its 3:30 AM and I’ve been up and about all day and hopes I can find your bed and complete the story.
