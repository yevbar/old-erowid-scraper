Newyear's Candyflip
Vitamin-C, Vitamin-B6, 5-HTP, Alcohol, Ecstasy, LSD & 5-MeO-DiPT
by Dextrose

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 1.0 g oral Vitamin C (pill / tablet)
  60 mg oral Vitamin B-6 (pill / tablet)
  150 mg oral Tryptophan - 5-HTP (pill / tablet)
  T+ 1:00 8 glasses oral Alcohol (liquid)
  T+ 1:30   oral MDMA (pill / tablet)
  T+ 9:00 0.75 hits oral LSD (blotter / tab)
  T+ 9:45 10 mg oral 5-MeO-DiPT (capsule)
  150 mg oral Tryptophan - 5-HTP (pill / tablet)
  T+ 12:00 150 mg oral Tryptophan - 5-HTP (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 62 kg


[21:00]
Ingests 2 pills Vitamin-C (500 mg. in each, selfdosing over 12 hours), 2 pills Vitamin-B6 (30 mg. in each) and 150 mg. 5-HTP.

[22:00]
Arrives at some friends, starts drinking booze and beers (there were plenty of alcohol).

[~22:30]
Starts eating of them pills. (3 Diamonds, an EZ-test first turned orange, then pitch black - definitly MDMA, and probably also amphetamine).

[~23:30]
Party's on, everybody is totally drunk, loud and most of us are drugged too.

[somewhere between 2 and 4]
We take off 8-10 persons to a new-years psytrance party.

[~45 mins. after]
Arrives at the party, i don't remember details but it ruled, and i danced my ass off.

[~5:45]
A girl gives me 2 blotters (Condoms, very good LSD!), after i give her 10 mg. foxy methoxy.

[~6:00]
My friend doesn't hesitate to eat the one, i eat 3/4 of my blotter, and give the last quarter to a friend. (I had eaten the 3 pills at that time).

[~6:45]
We can't feel if the acid has kicked in, because we are pretty MDMA-fucked, so we each eat a capsule of 10 mg. foxy methoxy (5-MeO-DiPT) to kick in the acid... It was about that time i ate another 150 mg. 5-HTP.

[~7:00]
I go to take a piss, and notice some minor visual distortions, 30 seconds after while i'm pissing, the visuals are accelerating, and from that point i have the most beautifull visuals i've ever had, definitly from the acid, but with a little of the foxy-visuals mixed in.

[~7:45]
I take off from the party with a couple of friends.

[~8:30-9:30]
We arrive 3 persons at my place, my MDMA-buzz is still present, but now clearly much more dominated by the hallucinogens.
I eat another 150 mg.'s of 5-HTP.

...we trip, talk, listens to music, and enjoys each others great friendly company.

We order some food at about 8 pm., i go online to post something on a webboard, IRC's a little, logs off, and watches a movie with my friends.

Sleeps at about 11 pm.

Absolutely the best new years eve i had, a clear +++!
Great music, good friends, nice set & setting, good drugs...

I've been told that the blotters (Condoms) are 185 mic. each, wich i believe is true. [Erowid note: Nobody really knows]

MDMA & LSD is wonderfull together, and the little fox on top of it, is a great combination - absolutely recommendable!

/Dex
