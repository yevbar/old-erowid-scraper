Fundamental #7: When using a strong psychoactive...take oneself "off duty" from responsibilities that might be interfered with (job, child care, etc.), and arrange for someone else to be "on duty".
Recreational Use
Quetiapine
by Cutlutch

 
DOSE:   oral Pharms - Quetiapine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 278 lb


I have read many reports on this site that discourage the use of quetiapine as a recreational drug. I however have had good luck with using it in smaller doses when I have no other psycoactive substances around. I have been prescribed seroquel for almost 3 years and I admit at first some of the effects are intolerable, however if I can overcome or get used to some of these effects it can become a more pleasureable expierience.

I have taken as much as 150mgs and still been able to got to work at the factory. I have also still been competent enough to work the heavy machinery present although I tend not to. I could be different from every last person who posted on quetiapine, but I have had fun and information is information.
