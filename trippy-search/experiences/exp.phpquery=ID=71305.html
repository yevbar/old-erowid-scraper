Chamomile
by User0000

 
DOSE: 1 bowl smoked Chamomile (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


Has a pot-like taste, and while harsh on the lungs, gave a pretty immediate sedative feeling, of relaxation. It is like the tea, which I usually drink strong, 2 bags, but stronger - or like a weak or low dose of pot. Definitely feel cool, and have the pleasant aftertaste I've been missing.

Smoked 1/2 the material from one of my tea bags.
