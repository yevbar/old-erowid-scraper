Adrafinil
by giga

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 300 mg oral Adrafinil
  T+ 0:59 300 mg oral Adrafinil
  300 mg oral Adrafinil

BODY WEIGHT: 65 kg


As I read the following I thought, wow you should try this, seems not that neurotoxic like amphetamine.

From the Smart Drugs News, Vol.3, No.8: 'Adrafinil selectively stimulates alpha-1 adrenergic receptors, increasing alertness and sensory vigilance. Unlike other stimulants, such as amphetamines, Adrafinil does not cause anxiety and agitation, nor does it induce a need for 'rebound sleep.' Many other stimulants act as re-uptake inhibitors, increasing the levels of various neurotransmitters in the synapses. Adrafinil achieves its effect by binding to norepinephrine receptors directly, a very specific mode of action which greatly reduces the amount of unwanted side-effects. Moreover, the effect of this drug appears to be localized in the CNS, not stimulating the peripheral system to a significant degree.'


Well I took 300mg, and waited about one hour. Nothing. Took another Tbl. with 300mg. Again nothing. Then I thought, well take one more, and if its still inactive resell it to someone else. So I took another 300mg.

Waited, and waited, and the only thing that happened was, I was getting extremely tired, but was unable to sleep. Very unpleasant.

So my suggestion is, for everyone who searches for something that keeps you awake, take Yohimbine-HCL instead - Not Adrafinil. This medication is in my opinion totaly useless. No idea who put this on the pharma market.
