Great Trip and Fun with Nitrous
2C-I & Nitrous
by Japes

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 25 mg oral 2C-I (powder / crystals)
  T+ 2:30   repeated inhaled Nitrous Oxide (gas)

BODY WEIGHT: 190 lb


I've just finished one of the best nights of my life. Sitting here wishing it wasn't over, I'm just going to type up what I wrote down on a sheet of paper. First trip journal I ever made.

9:50pm Split a 100mg bag of 2ci with four people. Dumped 2ci on a .1 scale and it didn't register. Split into 4ths, drank in gatorade, then licked the bag clean. Dose was ~25mg.

[Erowid Note: A volume amount of one powder doesn't necessarily weigh the same as the same volume of another powder. For substances active at such a small dose, eyeballing is particularly unwise. See related article]

10:00 Slight body numbness when walking around. Could be placebo effect.

10:10 Starting to feel warm, slightly naseous stomach.

10:24 Strong feelings of onset. Already starting to see some shapes morph and changing of colors. Smoked a blunt with a few friends, naseau was making me only take small hits.

10:52 All of the sudden very cold. Can't stop shaking. Feels like I can 'feel' or touch colors and are drawn to them.

11:10 Feeling sensitive to sounds so I crept into the dark with a glow stick. My buddy syays 'tell him this is INTENSE.' Trails are very evident, very bright colors.

11:40 Music is amazing. Running around with glow sticks.

12:06 Roommate grabbing the pillow and saying 'it feels like a cold frosty cloud.' Conversation is awesome. I am very concentrated on what I am doing.

12:15 Roy is still playing with the pillow and rubbing it all over his face. 'Me and the pillow are one.'

12:20 Nitrous is amazing. Best thing I have ever felt in my life. Can only be described as a really intense and extremely long orgasm. Louder = Better (talking about music).

1:08 Still feeling great. Music is distorted and feels like it runs through my body. 'Feel the vibes.' Time seems frozen. I have never felt so awake, so alert.

1:23 Ice Cube=Great. Sounds are much more sensitive then usual. Notes can be felt like deep bass, but with higher frequencies that generally are not 'felt' but only heard.

1:46 Everyone is very concerned with the journal. Would like to help future trippers across the globe. Wish there was more information about 2ci and other psychadelics. Perhaps I will write a book, a full length novel on trips and experiences. A book where every chapter is a new drug or experience.

From this point on I didn't write anything else in the journal until the end of the trip. I recapped the night.

2-3am included a lot of raving with glow sticks. The upbeat tempo of techo music feels really great to dance to. Trails looks really great and are the centerpiece. At one point I felt like a conductor of an orchestra with the light sticks and was swinging them furiously like I was controlling the beat.

3:30-4am Beginning to come down. Completely crashed by 4:30am. Slight headache which continues through 7:16am when I am writing this now.
