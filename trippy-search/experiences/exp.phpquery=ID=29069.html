5-MeO-DMT
by Dr_Green_Thumbxxx

 
DOSE: 7.0 mg smoked 5-MeO-DMT (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 270 lb


This was my very first time using this substance, and would say its definitly not my last time. I have spent many months researching this drug and its affects, and would reccomend doing so before trying it. I recieved a gram finally today after waiting a couple of weeks for it and had to try it out.

I meditated before trying this to lower my pulse and breathing pattern. After making a CD to trip out to (I reccomend something your familiar with, that has a slow calm tune to it, like Jefferson Airplane's White Rabbit, Beatles or PINK FLOYD!) I sat back in my water bed, listened to the music quitely for about 50 minutes, breathing slowly, almost in a sleepy state. I tried about 4 mg in a glass pipe with a butane lighter, but it dissolved into liquid and smoked for about 2 seconds, which wasn't enough to enhale, so i tried it again with about 5 mg., and the same result happened, so them i got out the candle and lit it, and put in about 12 mg. and put over the candle. Soon it began to make a little smoke, when i bent over to inhale, all of a sudden i could see the pipe fill up as i inhaled to my lungs, and just like everyone says, it hit me the second it was in my lungs.

I started feeling like i was seeing stars, just like when you hold your breath too long. As soon as i exhaled, it was over from there, gone to a different world, left earth, into a blackness as i closed my eyes. One thing I must really focus on when tripping is the breathing, at first it was a little hard to keep it slow and thats when i felt a little fear and regret from smoking it, but when i calmed down again, it wasa good trip from there.

Now i normally don't have too many visuals when using other psycidellics like mushrooms, except when i eat about 7 grams and have PINK FLOYD in the backround, but i was seeing swirls of blue and white as i was listening to Wheat Kings by the HIP. After six-seven mins. of intense tripping, i was back to reality, and as happy as a clam, it has now been about 25 minutes since my comeback, and its was great!!!
