First Real Addiction
Tramadol (Ultram)
by D

 
DOSE: 225 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 225 lb


[Erowid Note: Driving while intoxicated or tripping is dangerous and irresponsible because it endangers other people. Don't do it!]

My experience with Tramadol was basically an accident. My friend Kelly and I were at my apartment when he told me he had found something interesting in my medicine cabinet. We looked it up on the internet, and, after but a few moments of consideration, we both took a few of the pills. It was about 5 o'clock, and onset was slow and steady from about 45 minutes in.

After an hour and a half, everything was wonderful. Music was more enjoyable, conversation with my friend was a lot more interesting, and just kicking back in general felt really good. I had no trouble driving home, and once there, I went right to bed. The Tramadol didn't help put me to sleep like I thought it would, but instead made lying awake and simply pondering things almost euphoric. I was also pleased that when I woke up, I was still slightly affected. This lingering high lasted well into the school day, and I had some trouble concentrating on my work.

The only other negative notes I have about Tramadol are that I had slight trouble urinating on some occasions, and, after I used it frequently for about a month, I found I was slightly addicted and went through mild withdrawals. I should also add that Tramadol was my first foray into the world of pain killers. After I tried several other drugs, including Oxycodone and Hydrocodone, I found that I enjoy Tramadol more.
