My Seizures
Tramadol (Ultram)
by Rifter

 
DOSE: 400 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


Often, I seem to somehow run out of benzo's and opiates at the same time. Once in a while I try to remedy the opiate pains with tramadol. Bad idea. I knew that the threshold for seizures was raised with use of tramadol, but when in that pain I often ignore common sense and medical knowledge. I made this mistake and ended up taking 400mg of ultram. Hell, maybe I would have seized either way, but I ended having a seizure after eating the ultram.
