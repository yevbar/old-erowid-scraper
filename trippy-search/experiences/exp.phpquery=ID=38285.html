A Comparison
Tramadol
by Responsible Buzzer

 
DOSE: 150 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


As an occasional recreational user of substances that enhance my sense of well-being, I'd always rejoice at the ability to obtain any type of either hydrocodone or oxycodone. I came across several websites that offered Tramadol (or Ultram) or even Ultracet (Ultram + Acetaminophen), without a prescription, and with overnight delivery.

I started with straight tramadol since the Ultracet was much more expensive, and I figured that I could take a couple Tylenol with the Tramadol to basically replicate Ultracet.

I started with a dose of 150 mg. (three 50 mg. tablets). After about 30 minutes, I felt the initial rush that I always have felt with 'codone' family substances. Later however, the buzz got more intense and satisfying, but I found that I did not experience the extreme itchiness and spaced out feeling that I always have with 'codones.' Just a steady and pleasurable, happy and satisfied feeling, with my concentration levels more intense and my enjoyment of great TV and music extremely enhanced.

I like to call Tramadol 'hydrocodone light', which really means that it basically gives me the same buzz, but much better without the negative side effects.
